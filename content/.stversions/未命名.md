螺旋衍射透镜的相位分布：$\phi(r,\theta)=\mathrm{mod}\left(\dfrac{\pi r^{2}}{\lambda f}+l\theta\right)$

考虑左旋圆偏振，拓扑电荷 $-l$：$E_{1}(r,\theta)=A_{1}(r)e^{i\left(\frac{\pi r^{2}}{\lambda f}-l\theta\right)}\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ i\end{pmatrix}$ 

右旋圆偏振，拓扑电荷 $l$：$E_{2}(r,\theta)=A_{2}(r)e^{i\left(\frac{\pi r^{2}}{\lambda f}+l\theta\right)}\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ -i\end{pmatrix}$ 

假设 $A_{1}(r)=A_{2}(r)=A(r)$

叠加之后：$E(r,\theta)=E_{2}(r,\theta)=A(r)e^{i\frac{\pi r^{2}}{\lambda f}}\left[e^{-il\theta }\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ i\end{pmatrix}+e^{il\theta }\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ -i\end{pmatrix}\right]$

其中
$$
\begin{aligned}
\left[e^{-il\theta }\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ i\end{pmatrix}+e^{il\theta }\begin{pmatrix}1 \\ -i\end{pmatrix}\right]=\begin{pmatrix}
e^{-il\theta}+e^{il\theta} \\
i(e^{-il\theta}-e^{il\theta})
\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}
2\cos(l\theta) \\
2\sin(l\theta)
\end{pmatrix}
\end{aligned}
$$

得：$E(r,\theta)=A'(r)e^{i\frac{\pi r^{2}}{\lambda f}}\begin{pmatrix}\cos(l\theta) \\ \sin(l\theta)\end{pmatrix}$
